This plugin displays relevant promotion recommendations below post content and allows you add them in sidebar as a widget.

== Description ==

ADR Promotion Recommendations is a simple plugin that displays promotion recommendations based on page content. Highly relevant promotions are displayed automatically right below your post content. You can also drop it to your sidebar as a widget.

To display promotions, you need to register your site and email contact. Please go to https://www.adrelevantis.com/wp/plugin-register.php for details.

== Installation ==

1. Search WordPress Plugin Directory by typing "ADR Promotion Recommendations", click Install Now button, then Activate button.
2. Go to https://www.adrelevantis.com/wp/plugin-register.php to register your site.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Why should I use ADR Promotion Recommendations? =

We recommend promotions relevant to your content dynamically in realtime. We have millions of products that virtually match any content. The matched promotions seamlessly blend into your content and look like additional materials to your post. No more annoying ads. Generate revenue without sacrificing user experience.

= How do ADR Promotion Recommendations appear? =

Once your registration is approved, promotions display right below your post content.

You can also have promotions in sidebar. Just go to Admin page, click Appearance, click Widgets, drag and drop AdrCdp Widget to the sidebar.

= How can I make money with ADR Promotion Recommendations? =

You get 75% of revenue generated by promotion sales.
